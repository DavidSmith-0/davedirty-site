Methods:

  • Walk around your city and note businesses without websites
  • Google "[industry] + [city]" and check which have bad/no sites
  • Check Yelp/Google Maps listings
  • Visit Chamber of Commerce directories
  • Facebook local business groups

Best Industries:

  • Restaurants & Cafes (always need sites)
  • Construction & Trades (showcase projects)
  • Professional Services (lawyers, accountants)
  • Healthcare (dentists, doctors)
  • Local Retail (need online presence)

  • Send 50 emails/week
  • Expect 10-15% response rate (5-7 responses)
  • Convert 30-40% of responses to meetings (2-3 meetings)
